The Teachings of George Durrant

"George Durrant is one of the most loved teachers of our day."

Whether through the written or spoken word, he has inspired thousands over the years with his humorous touch and his spiritual depth. His children have created this website to allow others to enjoy George Durrant's works. This also relieves them from handling the many requests they receive for "George Durrant material." Enjoy.
